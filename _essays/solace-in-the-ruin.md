We return to the rubble because it remembers us. The city hums—half-light, half-forgotten—while a cigarette stitches the night with embered thread. [[concepts/metaphysical_rebellion|metaphysical rebellion]] is not an anthem but a posture: a refusal to capitulate to the indifferent. We persist—absurd and lucid.
