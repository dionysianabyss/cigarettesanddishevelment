A manifesto of tenderness under pressure. The Absurd Lover seeks lucidity without becoming unkind, holds devotion without illusion, and guards a softness that does not pretend to be safe. Writing filed under this archetype concerns love, fidelity, breakage, and how to remain honest in intimacy.
